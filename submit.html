What belongs in the Commons?

Any resource where a person with domain expertise used AI as a collaborator to make something useful for teaching, learning, research, or university operations. Interactive applications, lesson plans, prompt libraries, assessment rubrics, governance documents, AI policies, workflows, frameworks, and more. There's no minimum technical complexity—a well-crafted prompt library is as valuable as a full interactive application.
